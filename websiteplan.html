Optional domain: thinkdeep.org

Site Purpose

The site will serve as an interactive introduction to philosophy. It will provide profiles of major philosophers, present famous quotes and ideas, and allow users to explore different schools of thought. It will also include a form to share users' favorite philosophers or philosophical questions.

Scenarios

  • Who are the most influential philosophers in history?
  • What is the difference between existentialism and stoicism?

Color Schema

  • Primary (#2C3E50): Used for headers, navigation bar, and footer.
  • Secondary (#ECF0F1): Background and section containers.
  • Accent (#E67E22): Highlight buttons and important text.

Typography

  • 'Merriweather' - used for headings and quotes to give a classic, thoughtful tone.
  • 'Lato' - used for body text to ensure readability and modern aesthetic.
