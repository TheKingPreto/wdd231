Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information is collected on Think Deep and how it is used.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide: When you use our "Contribute" form, we collect the information you enter, such as your name, email address, and message, to manage and respond to your contribution.
  • Data stored on your device: To enable the "Favorites" feature on the Quotes page, we use your browser's Local Storage. This information (the IDs of your favorite quotes) is stored exclusively on your computer and is never transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Information

The information you provide through the contribution form is used solely for the purpose of improving the content of this website. We do not share your personal information with third parties.

This policy is effective as of August 12, 2025.
